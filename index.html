50+ Years of Organic Farming Excellence

Connecting Tanzanian Farms to Global Markets

Hems Agribusiness is a family enterprise with roots stretching back over 50 years in Sudan, now proudly operating in Tanzania as a bridge between smallholder farmers and international markets.

We specialize in sourcing, processing, and exporting premium organic agricultural products including spices, pulses, oilseeds and nuts. Our commitment to sustainable practices ensures we deliver products that meet the highest international standards while supporting rural communities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Your Questions About Our Agribusiness Operations

Get answers about our organic products, processing methods, export procedures, and farmer partnerships.

We specialize in processing and exporting four main categories:
1) Fresh & preserved fruits (mangoes, pineapples, avocados) and grains
2) Pulses & legumes (all types of beans, chickpeas, pigeon peas)
3) Oilseeds (sesame, sunflower, pumpkin seeds)
4) Nuts (cashews, groundnuts) and spices (ginger, cloves, cardamom)

All products are processed under strict hygiene standards compliant with TPHPA, HACCP, and ISO 22000 regulations.

Our quality assurance process includes:
- Training farmers in Good Agricultural Practices (GAP)
- Rigorous sorting and grading at our processing facilities
- Multiple cleaning and washing stages
- Controlled drying methods (machine, oven, or dehydration)
- Final quality inspection before eco-friendly packaging
- Full traceability from sourcing regions (Morogoro, Mbeya, Iringa, etc.) to final destination

Yes! We actively collaborate with Tanzanian smallholder farmers through:
- Training programs in organic farming and post-harvest handling
- Quality control guidance for international standards
- Fair trade purchasing agreements
- Support for farmer cooperatives in regions like Dodoma, Tanga, and Kilimanjaro
Farmers interested in partnership can contact our Dar es Salaam office at Plot 73/Block C, Mikocheni Industrial Area.

Markets: 95% of our products are exported to:
- Middle East (our primary market)
- Europe and Asia
- Regional East/Southern African markets

Packaging Options:
- Bulk food-grade packaging for manufacturers
- Retail-ready eco-friendly packs

- Complete export documentation and logistics support through Dar es Salaam port

Our sustainability commitment includes:
- Zero-waste operations (by-products used for animal feed)
- Eco-conscious processing and packaging
- Promotion of organic farming methods
- Water and energy conservation in processing
- Direct farmer support to improve livelihoods
