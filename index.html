50+ Years of Organic Farming Excellence

Connecting Tanzanian Farms to Global Markets

Hems Agribusiness Company Limited is proudly based in Tanzania, dedicated to delivering premium organic agricultural products to both local and international markets.

With a strong commitment to quality, sustainability, and community, we specialize in the processing and export of 100% organic commodities — including grains, pulses, spices, and oil seeds. Our mission is to showcase Tanzania's agricultural potential through ethical farming practices and global-standard products.
