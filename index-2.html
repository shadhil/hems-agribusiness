50+ Years of Organic Farming Excellence

Connecting Tanzanian Farms to Global Markets

Hems Agribusiness is a family enterprise with roots stretching back over 50 years in Sudan, now proudly operating in Tanzania as a bridge between smallholder farmers and international markets.

We specialize in sourcing, processing, and exporting premium organic agricultural products including spices, pulses, oilseeds and nuts. Our commitment to sustainable practices ensures we deliver products that meet the highest international standards while supporting rural communities.

Spices & Aromatics
