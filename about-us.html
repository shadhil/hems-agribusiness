About Us

Welcome to HEMS Agribusiness company Limited

From Tanzanian Soil to Global Tables – Naturally Nourishing

Hems Agribusiness Company Limited is a family business which has started way over 50 years back in Sudan, focusing on the drying and processing of high quality organic spices ,fruits and cleaning pulses{all types of beans,peas and corns etc} and all type of seeds{sesame seeds,sunflower seeds,etc} The company is proudly agribusiness enterprise dedicated to sourcing, processing, preserving, packaging, and distributing all of these high-quality agricultural products to international markets. With a commitment to food safety, sustainability, and fair trade practices, we serve as a reliable link between smallholder farmers and global consumers.

We specialize in a diverse range of organic food categories — from fresh farm produce to expertly preserved goods — ensuring year-round availability and extended shelf life without compromising nutritional value or flavor.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver premium organic foods through ethical farming and quality processing, empowering Tanzanian farmers while meeting global standards to nourish international markets across Africa, the Middle East, Europe and Asia.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become Africa’s leading exporter of premium-quality agricultural products by promoting sustainable farming practices, empowering rural communities, and connecting Tanzanian farmers to global markets.
