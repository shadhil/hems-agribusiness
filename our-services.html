Our Services

End-to-End Agribusiness Solutions: From Farm to Global Markets

At Hems Agribusiness, we deliver vertically integrated services that transform Tanzania’s finest organic produce into premium exports. With 50+ years of expertise, our meticulous processes span* sourcing, processing, packaging, and distribution — all designed to meet international standards while empowering local farmers. From hybrid drying technologies and eco-conscious packaging to transparent supply chains and strategic market access, we ensure every product retains its quality, authenticity, and competitive edge. Partner with us to experience the Hems difference: where sustainable practices, farmer collaboration, and global compliance converge.

Processing & Quality Assurance

Hems Agribusiness adheres to strict hygienic processing, complying with both Tanzanian (TPHPA) and international standards (HACCP & ISO 22000). Rigorous quality checks at every stage—from farm to packaging—guarantee traceability, consistency, and premium product quality.

Packaging & Export

We supply bulk and retail-ready packaged products for wholesalers, retailers, and manufacturers, offering eco-friendly materials, private labeling, and full export logistics support.

Sourcing & Supply Chain

Hems Agribusiness sources 100% organic products from Tanzanian farmers in regions like Morogoro, Mbeya, and Kilimanjaro. Our network ensures premium quality for global export after processing at our central facility.

Processing & Packaging Facility

  • Sorting & Grading – Precision separation by quality and size.

  • Cleaning & Washing – Thorough removal of impurities.

  • Controlled Drying – Machinery, oven, or dehydration based on product. needs.

  • Cutting/Slicing – Customized for fruits, vegetables, and spices.

  • Quality Assurance – Final inspection and accurate weighing.

  • Eco-Packaging – Compostable, food-grade bags for freshness and sustainability.
